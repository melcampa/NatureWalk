Mount Holyoke College Nature Walk - Foliage

Enjoy the Beauty of Foliage

Explore the vibrant colors of foliage along the nature walk. Here are some highlights:

  • Maple Trees: Witness the fiery red and orange hues of maple leaves.
  • Oak Trees: Admire the deep brown and golden tones of oak leaves.
  • Birch Trees: Enjoy the delicate yellow leaves of birch trees.
  • Pine Trees: Contrast the evergreen needles of pine trees with the autumnal colors.

Don't forget to bring your camera to capture the stunning views!
